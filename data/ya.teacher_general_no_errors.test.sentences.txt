shonte tsach yémotasacho'ta atthoch yerrmoera kochetholl ñeñth yeterrtsa ellerro
e'ñe kowen tomanta'tenet po'rretrat thañ atethka añorreth allempo womhchena
teropexhpa mamthothoch yetsepeñ tharromar emansa pamomtepa' awoch mópach
enkñapa' awath nakoch echenan powamek ollothcha yexhkatatenan papakell mapyo
arr yaxhtaretwerra all shontena apexhma'tsexhno o'llo chetso all setarwasen
all peyxhenapa' shontet se'nareten senorr ñeñcha tsomoen chashanesha
samathoxhtasto o'ch atsrrema ameseñethpa' o'ch chamas parraro amach sawanto
chetso napchaten poecharr yetspoemech amach samato allcha nétamech pathñoth
ñerram xho yeshollmechen ye'serpa nanakcha kowen yortatnom och yene' yexhnopho
awathekma newomhchataph amá phanapno ámata xho phatsrremoeno attho neshkap
awepatka moñech aspeneth ñeñthomaretka waterra womenk orayka yeporwerr arr
ñapa' awokaye pokwa poétomo allo to'tomata allempo asnerr o'llesrraromar
añpa' nanak ma'pcharrmoe' párraña tharrpa nanak ráket ñeñth pechorrena
ñapa' awokaye pokwa poétomo allo to'tomata allempo shoranem eskarelloth
wena apa' tepo xho otnenet awo' abellare'terret ñamha koshameterret posñesho
amat xha yellena ontappa' eshkënothe pechata ochek ñeñthomar wapa
llollopa' nanak tsopoen amacha attha emotena xhochxho tsopanem koñeth
serrparenten llollo errponthe nenan pame atollep ñamha pa'ñcho eshkenocha mornat
nerram esempo yetayena ontapopa añcha nanak yenten moeñ topñarero
allempo sanan rokëppa éño arr ete poemallanto xho sesatspe awo materra thekma
xhoch señotatannata ateth yépakllaten ñamha seyenpannata atthoch senwa sepakëll
otenet koyanesha arromatñanesha' ánoch eñweneto shopshotheñoch rromoet
ko'ch ñérra'm amá mecharropa' enteypa' o'ch sanapechey
ñeñth pós tsomoen wakëshpa' o'ka shotanem tsapathpa' po'poñcha yápamoeñ
oten llollo sétantena awathekma thetathe a'nosetensa shokshok o'ch semoshnerr
yá wasotatspa' nanakcha yerrmata'tannena tharromar yemorrenteñ yamots
ashenpa' alla rratseten tharro poetsatho all wankanmet moerllecho
allompo xho yorren kones chase' xhokmach yakerena acha' sa'pa'
ora enkanesha' ñeñth wetsa arr xhokmach yawenet poeposhpoeñoth thayemetsen
o'ch neposyawapa' o'ch sa'mothech samha naretnomoe momaph ñamha petokake
och awey temek yoch pa'och yethokena poporr all nenak reñ tome'
thpa' oka' otwa allcha yoñem nerrancha awey aspencho eshkeñoth amach yepayato
poshollo yeyxhaterre atthoch yetsomaten ñeñthomaro yerren yerrar a'potareth
añ mampa' ñeñthcha phapapoer péyey xho oten ñeñthoch akoshawerr
pa'monásheñpa' ñokopka' kellkëñ
amach awathekmotheñ semoene'the' thepa' xhoch sokwanen sekamkëñ parrocheye
sapa' arret seyxhena tharraña amá yechmetanneno yapa' tepotheye yepakllaten
all anets mapsato shonte yanesha ñeñth nanak és eñotets thayemetsen
e'ñe kowen neño't ñeñth peyxhateney ayxhatañ yomporcha phanate parasyos
xhoch phamethan errponmatthe ot pallerr allponcha nethoreta atthoch nokrrerra
llollopa' ellapen poñellatats ellerrenatsapa' atserrtena pañyaph amá rráneno
añcha ayoset mamats eshkeñocha pat ñeñcha yorwen ellerroneth

xhochka yokthanet thekma ñetpa' chemetenet arr thetathe yechenkëna o'ch yepayata
all seyxhenapa' añ sa'rrotampenaya' sap xhoch señota thetathe señoshena
